Tableau Analytics: Global Health & Music Industry Trends

🏗️ Project Overview
This project showcases data visualization and analytical techniques using Tableau Public, completed as part of the Data Technician Bootcamp. The analysis is split into two distinct tracks: a global health study using the Gapminder dataset and an exploratory analysis of the Spotify "Features" dataset.

1. Global Health Insights (Gapminder)
Objective: Acting as a Data Analyst for a global health organization to identify trends in life expectancy and population demographics to assist in future resource allocation.
Key Findings: * Regional Leaders: Europe consistently maintains the highest life expectancy globally.
Health Strains: Higher BMI rates recorded in Europe suggest an impending strain on national health services (such as the NHS), requiring targeted BMI reduction goals.
Disease Prevalence: Lung cancer is significantly more prevalent in France, Germany, and Italy, with men identified as a higher-risk demographic.

2. Music Feature Analysis (Spotify)
Objective: Exploring the relationship between musical attributes (energy, acousticness, duration) and track popularity/danceability.
Key Findings:
Danceability Correlation: Data indicates a negative correlation between song duration and danceability; essentially, the longer a song lasts, the less likely people are to dance to it.
Genre Insights: Discovered that "Acapella" tracks rank lowest in danceability metrics compared to other mainstream genres.

🛠️ Technical Skills Demonstrated
Data Visualization (Tableau)
Bar & Line Charts: Ranked continents by life expectancy and used Top N Filters to visualize the "Top 5 Countries" over time.
Scatter Plots: Analyzed the relationship between Average BMI and Life Expectancy to identify health clusters.
Pie Charts: Utilized Quick Table Calculations to show "Percentage of Total" for gender-based population distribution.
Interactive Dashboards: * Combined multiple worksheets into a unified "Global Health Insights" dashboard.
Implemented Global Filters (Country/Year) using "Single Value Dropdowns" for user-driven data exploration.
Analytical Thinking
Trend Documentation: Identified how demographic shifts impact public infrastructure.
Correlation Analysis: Used Spotify's technical attributes (Valence, Speechiness, Liveness) to draw conclusions about listener behavior.
📊 Project Deliverables
Worksheet: Life Expectancy by Continent (Sorted Bar Chart)
Worksheet: Life Expectancy Trend (Filtered Line Chart)
Worksheet: Population by Gender (Interactive Pie Chart)
Worksheet: Health Metric Comparison (BMI vs. Life Expectancy Scatter Plot)
Final Dashboard: "Global Health & Spotify Insights" – An interactive multi-page dashboard.
